Privacy Policy

Phily · Last updated: [28/06/2026]

In short: Phily runs entirely on your device. It does not collect, store on our servers, or transmit your personal information. Your photos, videos, and camera feed never leave your device except when you choose to save or share them.

Who we are

Phily (the "App") is a composition-aware camera developed by [Lanxin Yan] ("we", "us"). This policy explains how the App handles information. If you have questions, contact us at [lanxin.yan10@gmail.com].

Information we collect

We do not collect, sell, or share personal information. The App has no accounts, no sign-in, and no analytics or advertising trackers. We do not have servers that receive your data.

Camera & microphone

The App uses your camera to display live composition guides and to capture the photos and videos you take, and the microphone to record audio with your videos. This media is processed on your device in real time. It is not uploaded to us and is only saved or shared when you choose to.

Photo library

With your permission, the App accesses your photo library so you can browse and manage your shots in the in-app gallery, and saves the photos and videos you capture back to your library. The App does not upload, copy, or transmit your library elsewhere.

On-device processing

To suggest framing, the App detects subjects (such as faces) using on-device machine learning. This analysis happens entirely on your device, is used only to draw the live guides, and is never stored or sent anywhere.

Purchases & subscriptions

Phily Pro is sold through Apple's In-App Purchase system. Apple — not us — processes your payment and manages your subscription. We never see or store your payment details; the App only learns whether you currently have an active entitlement. Apple's handling of your purchase is governed by Apple's Privacy Policy.

Data stored on your device

The App stores a small amount of information locally on your device only — for example, the date your free trial began and whether you are subscribed — so it can apply your entitlement. Removing the App removes this data.

Children's privacy

The App is not directed to children under 13 and does not knowingly collect personal information from them.

Changes to this policy
